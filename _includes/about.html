Hi, I'm {{ site.author.name }} — a Business Analytics graduate from Macquarie University based in Sydney. My background combines a Master's in Business Analytics with a degree in Mechatronics Engineering, which gives me an engineer's instinct for data: where it comes from, where it breaks, and how insights reach business decisions.

Most recently at Green The Chain, I built GA4 traffic dashboards, implemented UTM tracking across outreach channels, and conducted supplier market research across 50+ ESG product categories. My earlier roles involved event data management, partner pipeline analysis, and reporting workflows across startup, sustainability, and business development environments.

My project work spans energy markets, transport, sustainability, and sports analytics — translating data into findings with clear operational and business implications, using Python, SQL, Power BI, Tableau, and Excel. I'm drawn to roles where data leads to decisions, not just reports.
