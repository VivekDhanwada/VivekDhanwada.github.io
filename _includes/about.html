About Me

Hi! I'm {{ site.author.name }}, a Business Analytics graduate from Macquarie University (WAM 70.13) based in Sydney, Australia. My background combines a Master's in Business Analytics with an undergraduate degree in Mechatronics Engineering, which means I think about data the way an engineer thinks about systems: how it is generated, where it breaks, and how insights reach the people who need them.

Most recently, I worked at Green The Chain, a climate-tech startup in Sydney, where I built GA4 traffic dashboards, implemented UTM tracking across outreach channels, and conducted supplier market research across 50+ ESG product categories. Before that, I supported data and event management for a 12,500+ participant sustainability event at Climate Action Foundation. Earlier in my career at AIESEC, I progressed from Business Development Manager to Head of Business Development and then National BD Consultant, leading local BD activity before supporting partner pipeline reviews and performance reporting across multiple city teams.

My core skills are in data analysis, dashboard development, and operational reporting, with experience across Python, SQL, Power BI, Tableau, R, and Excel. I am drawn to roles where data connects directly to decisions, whether that means tracking campaign performance, analysing operational patterns, or turning messy datasets into clear, usable insights that support practical business decisions.
